About


Welcome to the NACCTA website!

The North American College Chinese Theater Alliance (NACCTA) was formed in 2023 by MIT and Wellesley College students and the faculty advisor of the Wuming Theater Club (a multi-college Chinese student theater group based at MIT) to facilitate communication among Chinese student theater clubs at universities throughout the United States and Canada.

NACCTA’s mission is to celebrate the many theater groups in North America that carry on the tradition of China’s earliest modern dramatists who studied abroad and participated in Chinese theater productions at foreign universities in the early 20th century. Like them, we recognize that performing dramas in Chinese (both plays from Sinophone regions and plays from other countries in translation) is an opportunity to participate in a global community of theater makers, promote Chinese language and culture, foster greater Sino-Western understanding and friendship, and provide a supportive community for Chinese students living far from their homeland.

The purpose of this website is to facilitate intergroup communication by providing links to each member club’s website, a network for sharing resources, helpful information for starting or maintaining a CCT (college Chinese theater), and a space to celebrate past and upcoming productions. As such, NACCTA serves as both current resource and commemorative archive. If you are a member or alumnus of a CCT, you will find information here about the activities at your school’s club and how to connect directly to their leaders, as well as potential resources on arranging rights and royalties agreements, technical aspects of productions, marketing and publicity, and future NACCTA member events. If you are not directly involved in a CCT, you will learn where there is one near you so that you can attend their productions and support their efforts.

Please feel free to contact website and NACCTA founding organizers with any questions at urop2023fall@mit.edu
